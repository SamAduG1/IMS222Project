Frequently Asked

  • code What is your primary tech stack?

    On the frontend I work primarily in React and Next.js. On the backend I use Flask (Python) to build clean REST APIs. For databases I use PostgreSQL managed through Supabase, with deployments on Vercel (frontend) and Render (backend).

  • work What types of roles are you targeting?

    I'm actively seeking full-time software engineering roles starting Summer or Fall 2026. My focus is on full-stack development and product-focused engineering teams. I'm open to remote, hybrid, or in-person positions and I'm comfortable in both startup and larger company environments.

  • lightbulb_outline How do you approach a new project?

    I always start by asking "what problem is this solving and for whom?" before writing a single line of code. From there I design the data model, build out the backend API, then construct the frontend on top of it. Every project I build gets deployed to a real production URL, because I believe shipping live products is what separates learning from actually doing.

  • school Tell me about your capstone project.

    My senior capstone is a Multi-Sensor Data Visualization Toolkit built with Python, Dash, and Plotly for a health research partner at Miami University. It processes real-world sensor data from health monitoring wearables, applies machine learning anomaly detection, and presents findings in an interactive dashboard designed for non-technical researchers. We follow Agile methodology with two-week sprints and a GitLab CI/CD pipeline.
