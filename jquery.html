I'm a Software Engineering student at Miami University graduating May 2026. I build full-stack web applications using React, Flask, and PostgreSQL, and I deploy everything I make to real production environments. I care deeply about writing clean, maintainable code and designing experiences that feel intuitive.

Outside of coding, I stay active by going to the gym regularly, which helps me maintain the focus and discipline that carries into my work as a developer. I'm a big sports fan, especially basketball, which inspired me to build StatScout.
